# Handbook Quality Scorecard

## Rating Scale

- `seed`: discoverable guidance exists, but most enforcement is still manual.
- `structured`: the repo has stable entrypoints plus at least one mechanical checker or CI contract.
- `harnessed`: change selection, validation, and evidence are mostly repo-native and mechanically enforced.

## Domain Scores

- Backend: `structured`
- Frontend: `seed`
- SQL Integration: `seed`
- Docs and Translation: `seed`
- CI and Tooling: `structured`
- Generated and Extensions: `seed`
